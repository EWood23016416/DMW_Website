Plot Summary

You have gotten lost in a forest trying to outrun a horde of zombies pursuing you. You can see a building through the gap in a metal fence, you need to find a way to get past it and hide away until the zombies have left. The aim of the game is to kill/evade any zombies in your path, you will need to traverse the area finding a way to get around the gate and into the building. To open the gate there is 4 generators scattered throughout the level. They must all be activated to finish the level. Once past the gates the door into the church is locked, there is a key in the level that can be found at any time. To win the gate must be closed and you then must step foot in the church.

Genres and Theme

Immerse yourself in a tense zombie horror experience where survival means more than just pulling the trigger. Engage in an FPS combat experience, fending off relentless undead while navigating a forest filled with danger. Action-packed encounters keep you on edge, forcing you to think quickly and react faster. But brute force isn’t always the answer—puzzle-solving elements challenge you to use your surroundings, uncover hidden paths, and outsmart the horrors that lurk in the shadows. Every moment is a test of skill, strategy, and survival

Why Should you play this game?

This isn’t just another zombie game—it’s a test of your instincts, reflexes, and ability to stay calm under pressure. Every moment is filled with tension as you fight to stay alive, navigating the area, and make split-second decisions that could mean life or death. The world around you is unforgiving, forcing you to think fast, adapt, and use everything at your disposal to escape the horrors closing in. Whether you’re dodging danger, searching for a way forward, or facing the undead head-on, every step brings a new challenge. Will you make it out alive?
